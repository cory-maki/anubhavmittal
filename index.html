About Anubhav

Finance, Strategy & M&A Leader

Anubhav Mittal is a senior executive who has built his career at the intersection of finance and strategy — partnering with CEOs, boards, and operating leaders to drive growth and create lasting enterprise value. He currently serves as VP and Global Head of Business Development & M&A at Archer Daniels Midland (ADM), where he leads the company's global agenda across acquisitions, divestitures, joint ventures, and strategic partnerships. You can read more about his career in corporate development and M&A on his about page.

Over the course of his career, Anubhav has led or helped execute approximately $10 billion of transactions and strategic investments — spanning end-to-end opportunity assessment, valuation, diligence, structuring, negotiation, and value capture. His experience spans acquisitions, partnerships, carve-outs, disciplined capital allocation, and large-scale investment governance.

Before his current role, Anubhav served as CFO of ADM's Nutrition Business Unit — an approximately $8 billion global business spanning both B2B and B2C markets. He also held senior finance and strategy roles at Kellogg Company and Booz & Company. He earned his MBA from Harvard Business School and his B.Tech. from IIT Kanpur, graduating in the top 5% of his class. He holds the CFA and CMA designations.
